Intro Dive at Ras Mohamed National Park

A great way to see the national park of Ras Mohamed and swim or snorkel in its crystal clear waters.

Trip Number: 10
Price: 95$
Duration: 8 Hours
Book Trip

Trip Discription

Discover the beautiful underwater colors and marine life of the national park of Ras Mohamed and spend an unforgettable day, with intact land and sea. relax, unwind, and get a fantastic tan. You will be transferred from the hotel to the Marina then the boat will sail around 08:00 am Towards the national park of Ras Mohamed, where you will have the opportunity to explore the underwater marine life. Clients get their pre-booked intro dives, as it is a full diving experience with an instructor for 15 – 20 minutes. Then you will enjoy snorkeling the rest of the tour. A delicious buffet lunch including soft drinks will be served on board.

Inclusion
  • Hotel pickup by comfortable and air conditioned vehicle.
  • National park entry fees.
  • Diving and snorkeling equipment.
  • Buffet lunch. And Soft drinks.
Exclusion
  • Personal expenses.
Important notes
  • Pickup time will be sent to your email one day before the tour
  • need to get with you passports, towels, swimming wear, water Camera, and sun block.
Cancelation policy

Free Cancelation Up To 24 Hours Prior To The Tour Date.
